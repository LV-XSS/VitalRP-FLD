Legal Responsibilities

01

Biometric Storage Compliance

All firearms must be kept in an approved biometric-locked safe when not in immediate use. Failure to secure hardware results in immediate suspension.

02

Quarterly Ballistic Testing

License holders are required to submit their primary firearm for a state-mandated forensic marking update every 90 days.

03

Transport Notification

Movement of Class 2 or higher hardware across state lines must be logged via the Digital Officer Portal 24 hours in advance.

Mandatory Minimums

Under the 2026 Sovereign Authority act, any firearm misuse involving unverified ammunition will lead to a mandatory minimum of 5 years incarceration and permanent blacklisting from the Firearms Licensing Division database.
