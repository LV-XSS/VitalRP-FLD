The Firearms Licensing Division (FLD) serves as the primary regulatory body for the acquisition, possession, and transfer of firearms within the jurisdiction of Los Santos. Our mandate is to balance constitutional rights with the imperative of public security.

Utilizing state-of-the-art verification protocols and integrated databases, the FLD ensures that every licensed individual meets the highest criteria of legal and mental fitness.
